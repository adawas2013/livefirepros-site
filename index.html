Fireplace remodel costs vary based on scope, materials, and finish level. A cosmetic refresh with new tile and paint can start around $1,500, while a full structural remodel with custom stone, a new insert, and mantel work can range from $8,000 to $18,000+. We provide detailed, no-obligation estimates so you know exactly what to expect before any work begins.
Timeline depends on the project type. A simple fireplace surround update can be completed in 2-3 days. A full fireplace remodel typically takes 1-2 weeks.
