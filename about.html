Our Story

From Passion Project to
Premier Pros

LiveFirePros was founded by industry veterans with over 15 years of hands-on experience in the fireplace and outdoor living space. What started as a deep fascination with how fire transforms a home — turning a blank wall into a gathering place, or a bare patio into a year-round retreat — grew into a company dedicated to that single idea.

Based in North County San Diego and serving communities across Southern California, we've built our reputation one project at a time. Every fireplace remodel, every custom fire pit, every outdoor kitchen we build reflects our belief that craftsmanship isn't a buzzword — it's a standard.

We're not a franchise. We're not a gig-economy dispatch service. We're a tight crew of licensed specialists who genuinely love this work — and it shows in every project we deliver.
